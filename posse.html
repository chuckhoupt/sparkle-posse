Sparkle has a Posse: Demo Mode

Welcome to Sparkle Posse - the Sparkle Mac profile visualizer. This demo mode displays fake profile data consisting of all Mac models, OSs, languages, and random IPs. Models link to EveryMac and OSs link to Wikipedia release histories. GeoIP info is fetched from ip-api. Be sure to mouse over everything to see tooltip details.

If you just installed Sparkle-Posse on your site, get started by copying config-template.php to conf.php and setting up the path to your web logs.
